The Gnostic center

Gnosis is a greek word that means Knowledge, an inner knowledge that links us to our authenitic spiritual reality.

for many centuries , many Gnostic masters created spiritual traditions in order to help us guide us in the path of self-realization.

Some of these traditions are well known today and some remained in secrecy and are hermetic orders.

Some of the well known Gnostic traditions are:

  1. The cathars
  2. The Primitive Gnostic Christians
  3. the neoplatonic groups
  4. The Masons
  5. The tradition of Alchemy
  6. The rosacrucians and Martinists among many others...
